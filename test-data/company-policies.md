# Berghoffer Digital — Company Policies

## Refund Policy

All customers are entitled to a full refund within 30 days of purchase, no questions asked. Between 30 and 90 days, we offer pro-rated refunds based on usage. After 90 days, no refunds are issued but we can apply account credits toward future services.

Refund requests should be sent to billing@berghofferdigital.com. Processing takes 5-7 business days.

## Leave Policy

Full-time employees receive:
- 20 days annual leave
- 10 days sick leave
- 5 days personal leave
- Public holidays per local jurisdiction

Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance for planned leave. Sick leave requires a medical certificate for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

## Remote Work Policy

All employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote arrangements require manager approval and HR sign-off. Remote workers must be available during core hours (10am-3pm local time) and maintain a stable internet connection.

## Expense Policy

Business expenses are reimbursed within 30 days of submission. Receipts required for all expenses over $25. Pre-approval required for expenses over $500. Travel bookings must use the company travel portal (travel.berghofferdigital.com).

Monthly expense limits:
- Individual contributors: $500/month
- Team leads: $2,000/month
- Directors: $5,000/month
- C-suite: $10,000/month
